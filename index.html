How onAirVR Works

onAirVR App makes your mobile VR device act as a wireless HMD by getting real-time video/audio streaming which your game made with onAirVR Server renders on your PC.

Free From Cumbersome Wearables While Moving

In VR moving experience with positional tracking system, onAirVR lets players free from cumbersome devices like laptop backpacks. Only lightweight mobile VR headsets needed.
